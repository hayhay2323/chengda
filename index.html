01

What's the MOQ?

100 pcs per model for catalog items. OEM customisation starts from 200 pcs.
02

How long is the lead time?

45–60 days from deposit for catalog items; add 15–30 days for OEM tooling.
03

Which certifications do you hold?

CUPC (North America) and ISO 9001 in-house. WaterMark, CE, SASO available on request for OEM runs.

Can you customise shape, size, branding?

Yes — full OEM/ODM. Tooling from USD 3,000+; mould owned by the client. 3D approval before tooling is cut.

T/T 30/70 (30% deposit, 70% on B/L copy). L/C at sight for orders above USD 50K.
