Process

From brief to container.

Four stages, one Chaozhou facility. Direct contact with the production team at every step.

01 · Brief

Brief & quote

Share your reference, dimensions, target market. We respond with quote, MOQ and lead time within 24h.

02 · Sample

Sample & approval

Physical sample produced and shipped for review. Iterate until tolerances and glaze match exactly.

03 · Production

Tooling & production

Mold cut, slip-cast, fired at 1,250 °C, glazed and inspected. Batch traceability throughout.

04 · Logistics

Pack & ship

Custom packaging applied. FOB, CIF or DDP — your choice. Container loaded within 5 days of QC pass.
